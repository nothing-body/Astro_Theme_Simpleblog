## Things to Check Before Ordering

1. RackNerd's Terms of Service say Shared Hosting, Reseller Hosting, Hybrid Servers, and KVM VPS are delivered immediately after order and payment, while Dedicated Servers / Bare Metal Servers and domain registrations can take up to 72 hours.
2. Refunds are case-by-case because by default no refunds are provided once payment is received. Prepayments such as account credit are not refundable.
3. Cancellations require five days' written notice before the renewal date through the client area. Contract services require seven days.
4. Server data is removed immediately upon cancellation, so back up before submitting cancellation.
5. The TOS prohibits spam, copyright infringement / DMCA issues, excessive resource use, cryptocurrency mining, email spamming, bruteforce attacks, outbound DDoS, CPU mining, and Traffic Exchange.
6. VPS customers are expected to use Disk I/O and CPU in a fair-share, non-abusive manner. If you need to max out resources continuously, RackNerd recommends a dedicated server.
7. Dedicated servers include one complimentary OS reload per billing cycle; additional OS reloads in the same cycle cost $25.
8. RackNerd's SLA states a monthly 99.999% target for transit and electricity. Eligible credits must be requested within 30 days of the incident, and the account must not be past due.

## Who It Fits

RackNerd is a good fit for users who want many location choices, low-entry VPS pricing, and a self-managed hosting model. The annual entry KVM VPS plan works for small tests, lightweight services, and learning; Ryzen VPS is better when single-thread performance and NVMe I/O matter; Dedicated Servers are for users who need full hardware control, IPMI/KVM, higher transfer, or more IP addresses.

Before ordering, verify location, IPv6, operating system support, refund assumptions, cancellation date, SLA credit requirements, backup plan, and whether the promo code is still valid. Those details can affect the real cost more than the headline monthly price.
